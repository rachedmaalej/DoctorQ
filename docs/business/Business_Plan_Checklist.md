  - **Likelihood:** Medium (tech literacy varies)
  - **Impact:** High (no value delivered to doctors)

    - Pilot data validation: Track % of patients who scan QR vs manual add

- [ ] **Risk 2: SMS costs are higher than expected**
  - **Likelihood:** Medium (variable by provider and volume)
  - **Impact:** Medium (eats into margins)
  - **Mitigation:**
    - Pilot validation: Calculate actual SMS per clinic per month
    - Negotiate volume discounts with Twilio or local provider
    - Migrate 80% of users to WhatsApp (cheaper per message)
    - Set SMS limits per clinic (e.g., max 500/month)
- [ ] **Risk 3: Technical reliability issues (downtime, bugs)**
  - **Likelihood:** Medium (inevitable with new software)
  - **Impact:** High (damages reputation, causes churn)
  - **Mitigation:**
    - Implement comprehensive testing (unit, integration, E2E)
    - Set up monitoring and alerting (Sentry, UptimeRobot)
    - Create incident response plan (who to notify, how to fix)
    - Maintain 99% uptime SLA, or refund that month's fee

- [ ] **Risk 1: Can't scale support (too many clinics, not enough time)**
  - **Likelihood:** High (if growth accelerates)
  - **Impact:** Medium (service quality drops, churn increases)
  - **Mitigation:**
    - Build self-service knowledge base (FAQ, videos)
    - Hire part-time support agent at 30 clinics
    - Automate common support tasks (password resets, QR regeneration)
    - Set clear support hours and SLAs
- [ ] **Risk 2: Founder burnout (wearing too many hats)**
  - **Likelihood:** High (solo founder, multiple roles)
  - **Impact:** High (business stalls or fails)
  - **Mitigation:**
    - Outsource non-core tasks (accounting, design)
    - Set boundaries (no support after 6 PM, weekends off)
    - Bring on co-founder or early employee at 20 clinics
    - Build systems and processes to reduce manual work
- [ ] **Risk 3: Payment collection issues (clinics don't pay on time)**
  - **Likelihood:** Medium (cash flow challenges in SMBs)
  - **Impact:** Medium (cash flow problems)
  - **Mitigation:**
    - Require prepayment (monthly or quarterly)
